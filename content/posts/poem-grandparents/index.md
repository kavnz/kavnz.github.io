**G**uide to life \
**R**ainbows in the sunshine \
**A**wesome grandparents \
**N**octurnal at chores \
**D**irection-ist \
**P**arents' parents \
**A**lways calm \
**R**eliable \
**E**nthusiastic \
**N**ature lover \
**T**rustworthy \
**S**oothing
